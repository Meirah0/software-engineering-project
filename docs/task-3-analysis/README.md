# Task 3 – Analysis

## Project
Sukoon – Smart Hospital Support Application

Sukoon is a digital health startup concept designed to improve the hospital experience for patients and visitors. It reduces stress by providing navigation, appointment support, reminders, and AI-based emotional support.

---

## 1. Problem Definition

Hospitals are often stressful, confusing, and overwhelming environments. Patients and visitors face issues such as:

- Difficulty navigating inside hospitals
- Missed or delayed appointments
- Lack of emotional support
- Poor communication

This creates anxiety and reduces overall patient satisfaction.

---

## 2. Solution

Sukoon provides a smart digital platform that supports users through:

- Indoor hospital navigation
- Appointment booking and reminders
- AI emotional support chatbot
- Multilingual support

The goal is to create a calm and supportive hospital experience.

---

## 3. Target Users

- Patients
- Hospital visitors
- Elderly users
- International patients

---

## 4. Value Proposition

Sukoon offers:

- Reduced stress
- Better navigation
- Improved hospital efficiency
- Emotional support using AI

---

## 5. Market Potential

Digital health is a rapidly growing market. Hospitals are investing in:

- Smart systems
- Patient experience tools
- AI-based solutions

Sukoon fits perfectly into this trend.

---

## 6. Competitors

Existing solutions include:

- Hospital apps (limited features)
- Navigation apps (not healthcare-specific)

Sukoon differentiates by combining:

- Navigation
- AI emotional support
- Healthcare-specific features

---

## 7. Business Model

Possible revenue streams:

- B2B (hospitals pay subscription)
- Licensing software
- Premium features

## 8. Technology

Sukoon uses:

## 9. AI Integration

AI is used for:

This makes Sukoon more advanced than traditional apps.
