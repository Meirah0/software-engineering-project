# Mission – Sukoon

## Project Name
**Sukoon – Smart Hospital Support Application**

## Mission Statement
Sukoon aims to reduce stress, confusion, and uncertainty for patients and visitors inside hospitals by providing digital navigation, appointment support, reminders, and emotional AI assistance.

## Problem
Hospitals can be stressful and confusing environments, especially for patients, elderly visitors, first-time visitors, and people who feel anxious before or during medical appointments. Many users struggle with finding departments, remembering appointments, understanding where to go, and feeling emotionally supported.

## Solution
Sukoon provides a simple digital companion that helps users navigate hospital spaces, manage appointments, receive reminders, and access emotional support through an AI chatbot.

## Vision
To become a trusted digital hospital companion that improves the patient and visitor experience in healthcare environments.

## Core Values
- **Empathy:** The system should support users emotionally and practically.
- **Simplicity:** The application must be easy to use, even under stress.
- **Accessibility:** The design should support different age groups and user abilities.
- **Privacy:** User health-related data must be handled carefully and securely.
- **Reliability:** The system should provide accurate and dependable information.

## Target Users
- Hospital patients
- Hospital visitors
- Elderly users
- First-time hospital visitors
- Hospital staff supporting patient experience

## Product Principles
1. The app should reduce user stress, not add complexity.
2. The interface should be clear, calm, and easy to understand.
3. The system should support both practical and emotional needs.
4. Data privacy and responsible AI use must be considered from the beginning.
5. Each feature should be validated through clear success criteria.
