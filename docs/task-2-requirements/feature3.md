# Feature 3 – AI Emotional Support Chatbot

## Feature Title
AI Emotional Support Chatbot

## Iteration
Iteration 3

## Goal
The goal of this feature is to provide calm emotional support to users who feel stressed, anxious, or confused in the hospital environment.

## User Story
As a patient or visitor, I want to talk to a supportive chatbot so that I can feel calmer and receive simple guidance during a stressful hospital visit.

## Functional Requirements
- The user can open a chatbot screen.
- The user can type a message about how they feel.
- The chatbot provides calm and supportive responses.
- The chatbot can suggest breathing, grounding, or asking hospital staff for help.
- The chatbot should clearly avoid giving medical diagnosis or medical advice.

## Non-Functional Requirements
- The chatbot tone should be calm, supportive, and simple.
- The system should include safety boundaries.
- The chatbot should not replace medical professionals.
- The interface should feel friendly and not overwhelming.

## Requirement Attributes
| Attribute | Description |
|---|---|
| Priority | Medium |
| Status | Planned |
| Owner | Meirah |
| Dependency | AI response logic and safety rules |
| Risk | The chatbot may be misunderstood as medical advice |

## Acceptance Criteria
- The user can send a message to the chatbot.
- The chatbot responds in a supportive and non-medical way.
- The chatbot suggests contacting hospital staff in urgent situations.
- The chatbot does not provide diagnosis or treatment instructions.
- The conversation interface is easy to use.
